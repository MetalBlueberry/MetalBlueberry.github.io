Why This Blog

October 24, 2019 • edited April 10, 2020

I’m starting this blog to have feedback of my improvement over time.

It is not the first time

I’ve attempted several times to start a blog with educational content but I always give up after the first or the second post. I think it has something to do with my tendency to not publish anything that I don’t feel that is perfect, so this time I will try to just publish something.

To ease the work of having new content, I think I will write not only about “How-to-do” projects but also about my thoughts in a relaxed way. I think this post is a good example.

Track progress

I’ve been doing some introspection of my previous work and I’ve found it really interesting and inspiring. I will try to turn this blog in the perfect place for future introspection that probably will be useful for more people than just me.
